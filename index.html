Services

Following are the services that we provide

Exhibition

Exhibition is the retail branch of the film industry. It involves not the production or the distribution of motion pictures, but their public screening, usually for paying customers in a site devoted to such screenings, the movie theater.We Manage Filmz will be focusing on the value chain of a film’s cycle by showcasing or exhibiting films for the audience, in nontheatrical as well as theatrical venues.

Distribution

A quality distribution makes sure that the film is exhibited to a wider audience, which shall focus on ROI.Further, with overseas becoming a major territory and considerable costs associated with the same any savings in the distribution costs will be a great boon for film producers and distributors alike. We Manage Filmz with its wide distribution network across the globe shall be focusing on the commercial utilization of films.

Film Management

Film Management is a new term altogether in Indian film industry. Films are like projects in other industries, with a timeline and cycle and need a smart management to ensure a successful completion considering various factors, including cost of production, technical resources, human resources, positioning in a film’s value chain, efficient returns on investment etc.

About

We Manage Filmz is a unique platform for global networking of film professionals, which will open avenues for exhibition and distribution of films, made by, independent filmmakers.

  • Why The Need?

    We have observed that lots of films are produced everywhere in the world, but very few make it to the exhibition stage. As the theatrical market cannot absorb this growing number of films, distributors tend to focus on mainstream or crossover films made by established directors, with commercially successful actors.

  • Also, Distributors, In particular, are willing to acquire films of successful actors and directors and at the same time, reluctant, to acquire and exploit the rights of first feature films, as their directors are new and obviously, not yet have made a name for themselves in the market. But at We Manage Filmz, we believe that the role of first feature films to reveal new directors and actors, outside the constraints of commercial circuit.

  • On the industry side, since there is a gap between festival screenings and commercial releases; some distributors may be reluctant to acquire rights of films screened in film festivals in their domestic market if the local press has already reviewed the films and if part of the potential viewers attended the screenings.

  • There is a lack of collaboration between these two main actors (festivals and distributors), but we believe a new business model is possible: Our wish is to give better access to first feature films, to give young directors more visibility on the international market and to ensure a commercial distribution of those films worldwide.

Eligibility

Categories & Requirements

Eligible Categories

  • Full Length Feature Film (90 minutes and above)
  • Short Films (Up to 89 minutes)
  • Animation (Full Length and Short animations)
  • Documentary Films
Requirements & Terms
  • Films from Independent filmmakers are eligible.
  • Films should be available on both 35mm and digital format
  • Films should not have been previously shown over the Internet or broadcasted on TV or been commercially released (in theatres and/or on other media) outside India.
  • Films, which have been previously presented in a festival in their country of origin, are eligible for selection, provided the fact that no other screening was arranged after the national premiere, inside and outside the country.
  • Preferably, films should not have been previously presented at any festival or film event (except in the context of a film market) outside their country of origin, and especially not in the participating territories.

Selection Committee

People who approve your submission

The We Manage Filmz selection committee comprises of film professionals functioning as a Selection Committee, which is composed of the We Manage Filmz Partners with the support of well-known advisors. The Selection Committee’s decisions are based on an outright majority and shall be final.

Participation

What it means?

Participation

  • The We Manage Filmz invites films to participate to the We Manage Filmz project. Once selected, the film will be officially announced to be part of the We Manage Filmz Label.
  • We Manage Filmz partners and curators may communicate with filmmakers and rights holders regarding their films.
  • We Manage Filmz reserves the right to make no comment on the reasons for not selecting a film.

Participation to the We Manage Filmz project includes in particular:

  • Publication on the We Manage Filmzofficial website, on associated webpages, and on all kind of marketing contents associated to the project.
  • Screenings in the We Manage Filmz festival partners
  • Acquisition of the distribution rights by the We Manage Filmz distribution partners

Special conditions

Conditions you need to adhere to

No film, selected for the We Manage Filmz Label, may be withdrawn from the project after the Rights holder has officially confirmed its participation.

Once a Film is submitted to the Selection Committee, it is understood the Rights Holder has accepted the We Manage Filmz terms and conditions.

More specifically, in case the film is confirmed to participate to the We Manage Filmz project, the Rights Holder commits:
  • Toconsider the national and international premiere of the film to the festivals if required.
  • Not to request screening fees to the We Manage Filmz festivals for the screenings of the film.
  • To provide We Manage Filmz festivals with the requested screening material.
  • To give a first option of purchase of the Film to the We Manage Filmz distributors in each participating country (all rights).
  • Not to request minimum guarantees for the purchase of the Film to the We Manage Filmz distributors outside the framework of the sales estimations.

WMF Funds

How we generate funds?

It is agreed by all the parties that a commission of 15% on all gross receipts incomes generated by the sale of a We Manage Filmz film within the framework of the project and will be automatically transferred to a dedicated We Manage Filmz bank account.

Depending on the requirements, the fund should be used in order to financially support specific activities such as:
  • The promotion of the We Manage Filmz Label and/or of the films in the international markets
  • The participation of films in the We Manage Filmz festivals
  • The P&A costs associated to the distribution of We Manage Filmz films
  • The organization of workshops with We Manage Filmz partners and/or the participation We Manage Filmz partners in existing workshops
  • The development of We Manage Filmz directors’ upcoming projects.

Submission Process

How you can submit your film?

  • Films will only be considered for selection after the entry form, duly filled and signed, is received by the We Manage Filmz, and in certain cases after the entry fee is received.
  • The We Manage Filmz has the right to settle any cases not covered by the regulations.
  • Films are to be submitted in their original version with English subtitles for the selection process.Films in English do not require translation.
  • It is possible to submit a working copy of the film. When filling out the entry form, the Rights Holder should state if he is submitting the final version or a working version.

Submission Fees
Right Holder has to pay 25000 INR as submission fee along with the form to We Manage Filmz.

Deadlines
We Manage Filmz accept entries throughout the year.

Entry form
Any submission of a film must include the Entry form duly filled up and signed by the authorized Rights Holder. Rights Holders must return the Entry form duly filled and signed to info@wemanagefilmz.com
